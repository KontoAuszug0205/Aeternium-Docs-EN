## 🛠️ General QoL Improvements
- Condenser costs adjusted to 3 / 6 / 9 / 12
- Maximum stack size of commonly used materials increased from 9,999 → 100,000
- Transport capacity per transport rank: 100 / 200 / 300 / 400 / 500
- Inventory pouches buffed: +3 / 6 / 9 / 12 slots
- Significantly increased map discovery radius
- Pal resurrection cooldown in the Palbox reduced (2:00 minutes)
- Hold button to hatch eggs removed (eggs can now be opened instantly)
- Fishing minigame difficulty slightly reduced
- All stat point limits removed (including elixirs)
- Interaction time reduced for:
    - Golden Chests on oil platforms (halved)
    - Junk scraps in the overworld (halved)
    - The vanilla spawn behavior of Pals has been adjusted so they no longer appear only within a very small radius around the player.
    - As a result, the world feels much more alive, open landscapes feel more natural, and views from higher elevations are no longer empty or lifeless.
